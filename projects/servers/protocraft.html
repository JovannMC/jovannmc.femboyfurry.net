Protocraft

Protocraft is a minigames server with a majority of minigames being built from the ground up. It is owned by ZuriProto with the help of many of his friends.
It is on 1.18.2 and also supports Bedrock clients to connect through Geyser.
I am currently recreating some of the minigames as Spigot plugins, mainly IslandTurfs and Deep Dungeons.
