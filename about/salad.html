Salad

Salad is a free program that allows you to share your PC resources to earn you money.
There are many different ways to utilize your PC, from cryptocurrency mining with your CPU or GPU, to sharing your internet bandwidth, to even sharing your PC's resources to real companies!
These companies pay Salad for the hardware they need, then run workloads like AI training or 3D rendering on your hardware.
After the job is complete, you will be paid for your PC's hard work.
If you would like to know more about that (SaladCloud), click here.

I've been using Salad for almost 3 years now and currently an Affiliate Applicant and Junior Moderator for the SaladChefs Discord server.
In those years I've earned over 1k USD which allowed me to fund stuff like my PC parts :)
I've also created some content related to Salad which you can check out here.
